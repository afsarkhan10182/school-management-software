Quotation for School Management Mobile App

Project Overview

This document outlines the estimated costs for developing a comprehensive School Management mobile application for both Android and iOS platforms. The system will provide a seamless, native experience for students, teachers, and parents.

Assumption: This quotation covers the mobile application (front-end) development only. It assumes that a complete backend with all necessary APIs will be provided and managed separately.

4. Cost Justification

  • Mobile-Focused: This estimate covers the complete design and development of native Android & iOS mobile apps.
  • Effort-Based: A total of 320 hours of skilled work, transparently mapped to each mobile feature.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Includes UI/UX, branding, development, testing, and app store submission.
  • Market Aligned: Similar native app projects are highly competitive at this price point.
  • High Value: Provides a robust, scalable, and modern mobile experience for all school stakeholders.
