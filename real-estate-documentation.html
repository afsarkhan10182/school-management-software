Real Estate Construction & Property Management System

This document outlines the key features of the Real Estate Construction & Property Management System.

1. Multi-Project Real Estate Management

  • Create new real estate projects like apartments, villas, or commercial blocks.
  • Assign teams, timelines, and budgets to each individual project.
  • Track sales, construction, and financials separately per project.
  • Compare project performance in terms of revenue and completion.
Operate multiple developments simultaneously while keeping each one well-organized and traceable.

2. Detailed Unit & Inventory Management

  • Define every unit’s size, features, floor, facing, and pricing.
  • Tag special units like corner, park-facing, or penthouse.
  • Real-time tracking of which units are available, sold, or blocked.
  • Search inventory by custom filters during client demos.
Maintain absolute control over your unit availability and pricing structure.

3. Customer & Lead Lifecycle Management

  • Store customer inquiries and manage them from first contact to closure.
  • Assign leads to sales agents and track follow-ups and meeting notes.
  • Store documents like ID proof, PAN, booking forms under each lead.
  • Improve conversion with an organized sales CRM process.
Build strong customer relationships from inquiry to possession.

4. Customized Payment Plan Management

  • Set custom payment milestones like booking, foundation, and possession.
  • Auto-send payment reminders based on milestone dates.
  • Track incoming payments and automatically update balances.
  • Generate receipts and collection reports instantly.
Ensure smoother collections with smart milestone-based tracking.

5. Receivables & Outstanding Payment Tracking

  • See which clients have due or overdue payments in one view.
  • Filter by date, project, or unit to locate pending payments.
  • Send due reminders via SMS or email.
  • Maintain up-to-date receivable records.
Track every rupee due and boost timely collections.

6. Construction Inventory & Material Management

  • Track all raw material purchases with invoice references.
  • Assign materials to construction phases and locations.
  • View current stock status and reorder low items.
  • Control wastage and optimize site procurement.
Control project cost by managing materials smartly.

7. Expense & Vendor Management

  • Record all expenses like contractor fees, permits, marketing, etc.
  • Manage vendor profiles, quotations, and outstanding dues.
  • Tag expenses by project and cost head for analysis.
  • Export vendor-wise or month-wise expense reports.
Stay within budget while maintaining full vendor traceability.

8. Financial & Profitability Reporting

  • Track income vs expense across projects.
  • Generate P&L reports per site or overall.
  • Visual dashboards for sales, bookings, and revenue.
  • Export detailed reports in Excel or PDF formats.
Make strategic business decisions with deep financial insights.

9. Document & Compliance Management

  • Upload approvals, blueprints, agreements under structured folders.
  • Separate compliance docs like RERA, licenses, and tax returns.
  • Restrict access to sensitive documents by role.
  • Maintain checklists for handover and legal clearance.
Go paperless with secure and searchable document storage.

10. Role-Based Access & Staff Control

  • Create user roles like Sales, Finance, Site Engineer, Admin.
  • Grant module-wise access and control visibility.
  • Log user activity and actions for accountability.
  • Protect data integrity with permission-based control.
Run operations securely with controlled staff access.

11. Business Dashboard & Real-Time Overview

  • Visualize sales pipeline, unit status, and collection progress.
  • Switch views between projects or time periods.
  • Mobile-ready dashboard for access anytime, anywhere.
  • Stay updated with real-time alerts and insights.
Get total visibility across your business in one dashboard.

12. Voucher Management

  • Create, manage, and track payment vouchers for all financial transactions.
  • Attach supporting documents and maintain a digital record for audits.
  • Streamline approval workflows for expense and payment vouchers.
Voucher management ensures every transaction is properly documented and approved, supporting transparency and compliance.
