School Management System

This document outlines the key features of the School Management System, including multi-branch management, role-based access, student and teacher management, exams, fees, and more.

User Management

  • Multi-branch system with SuperAdmin control and branch-specific data isolation.
  • Role-based access control (SuperAdmin, Branch Admin, Staff, Teachers, Students, Parents).
  • Custom domain and URL support for each branch with configurable login panels.
  • Secure user registration, profile management, and role-specific dashboards.
  • Comprehensive user management with add, edit, and remove capabilities.

Academic Management

  • Manage academic years, terms, class levels, programs, and subjects from a unified admin panel.
  • CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for all academic entities.
  • Organize students into year groups and classes for better academic tracking.
  • Frontend provides intuitive forms and lists for managing academic structures.

Student Information & Management

  • Complete student lifecycle management from registration to graduation.
  • Customizable student profiles with document uploads and sibling information.
  • Student promotion system with history tracking and multi-class support.
  • Leave management and student directory with advanced search capabilities.
  • Admin tools for student record management including disable/enable functionality.

Teacher Management

  • Admins can add, view, and manage teacher accounts.
  • Teachers have profile pages and can update their information.
  • Teacher directory with search and filters for easy management.

Examination & Results

  • Multiple exam setups (Marks, GPA, Marks & GPA) with scheduling and status management.
  • Online exams with CSV import, position/rank management, and exam fee payments.
  • Comprehensive report cards with customizable remarks, principal/teacher comments, and progress tracking.
  • Exam results publication controls and support for multiple exam types including tabulation sheets.
  • Advanced result dashboards and analytics for students and teachers with cumulative performance tracking.

Attendance

  • Subject-wise attendance tracking
  • Comprehensive attendance reports including daily overviews, percentages, and half-day options.
  • Smart attendance system with automatic holiday status and event-linked attendance management.
  • Role-based attendance access - teachers manage records, admins view all, students see restricted views.
  • Advanced attendance analytics with holiday integration and flexible recording methods.

Fee Management

  • Comprehensive fee collection system with multiple fee types
  • Advanced fee management with SMS reminders, fee revert functionality, and carry-forward due fees across sessions.
  • Professional fee processing including multiple receipt copies, customizable invoices, and payment ID management.
  • Complete fee tracking and reporting system with office accounting integration and comprehensive payment analytics.
  • Automated fee workflows with reminder systems, payment tracking, and detailed financial reporting capabilities.

Communication & Notifications

  • Whatsapp chat integration.
  • Email triggers for admissions and other events.
  • SMS notifications for live class schedules and other alerts.
  • Login activity reports.

Academic & Teacher Tools

  • Homework assignment and submission with SMS notifications.
  • Online admission with customizable and configurable fields and payment status.
  • Teacher schedules and restrictions on some operations.
  • Subject-wise attendance and exam remarks.
  • Quick creation of exam and class schedules.

Transport Management

  • Manage school transport routes and vehicles.
  • Assign students to specific routes.
  • Custom domain and URL support for branch transport modules.

Class Routine/Timetable

  • View and manage class routines and timetables.
  • Helps students and teachers stay organized.

Reports & Analytics

  • Centralized, role-based access to comprehensive reports across admissions, attendance, academics, finances, and operations.
  • Advanced filtering capabilities by branch, date, class, and custom parameters with export options (Excel/CSV/PDF).
  • Comprehensive report types including admissions, attendance, examinations, fees, communications, events, and inventory tracking.
  • Interactive dashboard visualizations with trend analysis for academics and financial performance metrics.
  • Secure branch data isolation ensuring confidentiality while providing detailed analytics and print-friendly report formats.
