School Management System

This document outlines the key features of the School Management System, including multi-branch management, role-based access, student and teacher management, exams, fees, and more.

Multi-Branch Management

  • Centralized SuperAdmin control over multiple schools/branches.
  • Branch Admin manages branch-specific data, staff, and students.
  • Data isolation between branches for confidentiality and security.
  • Custom domain and URL for each branch (examples provided).

User Roles & Access

  • SuperAdmin with full system control.
  • Branch Admin and Staff with restricted access.
  • Student and Parent panels with configurable login enable/disable.
  • Receptionist and other staff roles with specific permissions.
  • Role and permission management system.

User Management

  • Admins, teachers, and students can securely register, log in, and manage their profiles.
  • Role-based access ensures each user sees only the features relevant to them.
  • Admins can manage all users, including adding, editing, or removing accounts.
  • Modern dashboards and navigation tailored to each role.

Academic Management

  • Manage academic years, terms, class levels, programs, and subjects from a unified admin panel.
  • CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for all academic entities.
  • Organize students into year groups and classes for better academic tracking.
  • Frontend provides intuitive forms and lists for managing academic structures.

Student Information & Management

  • Student registration, profile management, and document uploads.
  • Customizable fields for student and admission data.
  • Multi-class student handling.
  • Sibling information displayed on student profiles.
  • Student promotion with history tracking.
  • Disable student functionality with a reason.
  • Student leave and leave request management.
  • Student directory with search and filtering options.
  • Admin tools for editing or removing student records.
  • Student promotion management.

Teacher Management

  • Admins can add, view, and manage teacher accounts.
  • Teachers have profile pages and can update their information.
  • Teacher directory with search and filters for easy management.

Examination & Results

  • Multiple exam setups (Marks, GPA, Marks & GPA).
  • Exam scheduling and status management.
  • Position/rank management in exams.
  • Online exams with CSV import of questions.
  • Report cards with customizable remarks and principal/teacher comments.
  • Exam progress reports showing cumulative averages and positions.
  • Exam results publication with enable/disable options.
  • Online exam fee payment feature.
  • Support for multiple exam types including tabulation sheets.
  • Result dashboards and analytics for students and teachers.

Attendance

  • Subject-wise attendance tracking.
  • QR Code attendance addon support.
  • Daily and overview attendance reports.
  • Attendance percentage in reports.
  • Half-day attendance option and automatic holiday status.
  • Attendance linked to events and holidays.
  • Student panel attendance view with restrictions.
  • Attendance records can be managed by teachers and viewed by admins.
  • Manual and automated (RFID/QR/Barcode) attendance recording.

Fee Management

  • Student fee collection with multiple fee support.
  • Offline and online fee payment gateways including Razorpay, Paytm (India), Flutterwave, Toyyibpay (Malaysia), Payhere (Sri Lanka), Midtrans (Indonesia), Jazzcash (Pakistan), SSLcommerz (Bangladesh).
  • Fee reminders with SMS and DLT template support.
  • Fee revert feature linked with office accounting.
  • Carry-forward due fees across sessions.
  • Multiple receipt copies printing.
  • Invoice and payment ID customization.
  • Manage and track student fee payments.
  • Generate receipts and payment reports.

Communication & Notifications

  • Bulk SMS and email scheduling with customizable templates.
  • SMS gateway support (Twilio, Msg91, Bulksmsbd, etc.).
  • Whatsapp chat integration.
  • Email triggers for admissions and other events.
  • SMS notifications for live class schedules and other alerts.
  • Login activity reports.

Academic & Teacher Tools

  • Homework assignment and submission with SMS notifications.
  • Online admission with customizable and configurable fields and payment status.
  • Teacher schedules and restrictions on some operations.
  • Subject-wise attendance and exam remarks.
  • Quick creation of exam and class schedules.

Transport Management

  • Manage school transport routes and vehicles.
  • Assign students to specific routes.
  • Custom domain and URL support for branch transport modules.

Class Routine/Timetable

  • View and manage class routines and timetables.
  • Helps students and teachers stay organized.

Accounting & Payroll

  • Office accounting integration for fees and salary payments.
  • Staff advance salary and leave requests.
  • Employee payslips with global logos.
  • Opening balance and automatic fines for late payments.
  • Salary payment management.
